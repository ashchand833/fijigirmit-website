Keeping the story alive

Fiji Girmit Foundation New Zealand

The Fiji Girmit Foundation New Zealand honours the journey, sacrifice, and achievements of the Girmitiya and their descendants. The Foundation keeps this history visible through remembrance, education, cultural events, and community connection.
